# Orange Cinematic Demo Video Design

## Style Prompt

A cinematic, dark, quiet product film for Orange, a persistent memory fabric for AI-powered teams. The frame should feel like a near-black control room where context travels between work tools and a glowing memory core. Motion is deliberate and sparse: pulse, type, reveal, cut to black, repeat. No marketing clutter.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#080810`
- Near-black surface: `#0F0F14`
- Primary accent: `#F97316`
- Amber glow: `rgba(249, 115, 22, 0.55)`
- Primary text: `#FFFFFF`
- Secondary text: `#6B7280`
- Dim border: `rgba(255,255,255,0.06)`

## Typography

- Primary: Inter, light to medium weights
- Monospace accents: ui-monospace / SFMono-Regular

## Motion

- Cinematic cuts, fades, pulse ripples, and scroll-story-like scene reveals
- Typewriter reveals should feel precise and quiet
- Orange pulse interstitials should feel like a heartbeat

## What NOT to Do

- No bright rainbow gradients
- No stock illustration style
- No playful stickers or cartoon UI
- No decorative grid/noise texture
- No dense marketing copy
